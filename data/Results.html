Of the NPM scripts, here are the relative amounts of each type of dependency. Most are very low frequency (as seen by the low mean), but there are a few that are completely out of the ordinary... one file lists all NPM packages as major version ranges... this seems to be the entire point of this project. If you look at the 95th and 99th percentiles, that will give a clearer indication of what a high value usually looks like for that group. Looking at the difference between observed variables for notjsonnum vs the other files, that shows the number of misformed files - about 0.7% were not able to be processed because of this.
